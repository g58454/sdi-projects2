Project 1

In this program, a tiny ball by the name of "Chomp" lives and roams free, without a care in the world.

He is a lovable character that wouldn't hurt a fly, unless that fly wants a fight.

Chomp has three choices; run, attack, and stun. By chosing one of these three options will determine the fate of the little bug.
